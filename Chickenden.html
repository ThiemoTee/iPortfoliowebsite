Project description:

The goal of this project was to realise a project from start to finish according to the agile work methods. This started with meetings with our client that gave us an assignment. Which was that he has a classic chicken den and since he has a busy live he wants a method to make it as autonomous as possible. Here from out we brainstormed a solution and kept the client in the loop to ensures he gets a solution he is happy with. What we came up was an automatic gate that opens during the day time to make sure the chickens are safe from predators during the night times. Besides this he wanted to have a automatic feeder for the chickens which he only has to refill every couple days and to finish it would be a nice to have any kind detection for when the chicken lays eggs. My main task was to create the automatic feeder which I realised by simulating a scale with a potentiometer which will open a hatch once its go below a certain value. Once this setup worked I supported my team with the other task.
